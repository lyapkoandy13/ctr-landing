Carpathian tourist road

Mobile application designed for tourists and travelers in the Carpathian region

Advantages of mobile application

Extremely easy to use
Supports IOS and Android
All information and photos of objects in one place
You can get a route to each object

Use without charge

The application is free for users and allows to quickly identify the most interesting cultural heritage objects nearby, as well as to pave the shortest route to them.

The application was developed within the project

"The World of Carpathian Rosettes - Activities for Preserving the Cultural Uniqueness of the Carpathians"

ENI Cross-border Cooperation Programme Poland-Belarus-Ukraine 2014 -2020 in order to improve tourists' access to the architectural and cultural heritage of the Carpathian region.
